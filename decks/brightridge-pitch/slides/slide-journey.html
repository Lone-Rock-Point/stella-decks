Actions
Hears about BrightRidge from a neighbor or search result. Visits the site for the first time.
Browses plan options, compares speeds and prices, reads nutrition labels, weighs against competitors.
Enters address to confirm availability. Selects a plan. Starts enrollment.
Creates account in SmartHub. Schedules installation. Sets up auto-pay.
Manages account, pays bills, contacts support. Considers upgrading or referring a neighbor.
Experience
Hopeful Uncertain Frustrated Confused Satisfied
Pain Points
Slow load on mobile and a cluttered homepage make it hard to understand what's being offered or where to go first.
Pricing shown in one place doesn't match another. Plan options don't map to real-world use cases. Spanish-speaking visitors have no clear path to content in their language.
Competing CTAs create confusion without hierarchy. Language support disappears on mobile. A failed address lookup leaves customers with no next step.
Enrollment hands off to a visually foreign environment with its own login. Nothing signals that customers are still in the right place.
Account management lives outside the familiar brand. No clear path to new plans or upgrades. No clear path to referrals.
Opportunities
Fast, mobile-first entry. Compelling above-the-fold value prop. Clear community identity.
Unified pricing across every touchpoint. Integrated bilingual experience. Clear navigation to the right plan.
Single focused CTA per page. Consistent pricing throughout. Language-persistent browsing for every visitor.
Warm visual handoff to SmartHub. Enrollment guidance that maintains brand continuity across the threshold.
Referral program. Proactive upgrade prompts. Improved self-service account management.
