Options Considered

The three options we weighed.

01
Accept the offer as written.
PROS: Clean exit. Removes execution risk. Earn-out preserves upside if we hit targets.
CONS: No market test. If competing bids exist, we leave value on the table. Earn-out terms favor Pendant.
02
Counter-propose terms within the window.
PROS: Addresses earn-out risk. Could buy time for a partial market check.
CONS: Pendant has signaled the offer is "best and final." Counter could end the dialogue entirely.
03
Decline the exclusivity and run a 60-day process.
PROS: Surfaces market value. Preserves optionality. Re-engages the H1 inbounds we couldn't pursue.
CONS: Pendant may walk. Process distracts ops. 60–90 days of management attention.
