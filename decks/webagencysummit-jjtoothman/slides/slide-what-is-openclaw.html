What is OpenClaw?

A self-hosted control plane for your AI assistant.

Open-source. Runs on your machine. Coordinates between models, messaging platforms, tools, and persistent memory.
“The agent itself is a collection of files on disk rather than code you have to write.” openclaw.ai
Multi-model
Claude, OpenAI, local models. The gateway routes — you don't switch tools.
Multi-channel
Slack, WhatsApp, Telegram, iMessage, Signal. DMs and group chats.
Identity-as-files
Personality lives in SOUL.md. Skills auto-discovered from the workspace.
Always on
24/7 background operation. Not a chat window you open.
