What I was after

Five things separate a tool from a teammate.

Persistence.
It remembers what you've discussed, decided, and built together. Not session-by-session amnesia.
Identity.
A defined role. A personality. A way of operating that's recognizable across interactions.
Tools.
It can actually do things — send the email, post the update, file the ticket. Not just talk about doing them.
Autonomy.
It works independently between prompts. Not waiting on me to ask the next question.
Accountability.
A scope of responsibility. Things that are hers to own. Not just a prompt window I happen to be using right now.
