Middle-Mile Grant Availability Map

The Middle-Mile grant project is funded by American Rescue Plan, Capital Projects Fund under a Grant Contract with the State of Tennessee. This grant will provide access to BrightRidge Broadband’s nation-leading speeds up to 10 GB symmetrical in a swath of western Washington County and the northeast corner of Greene County, including rural Fall Branch, South Central, Sulphur Springs-Bowmantown-Leesburg and west to the Greene County line north of Highway 11E, portions of Lamar, Dry Creek and Conklin, areas south of Jonesborough and areas south of Gray. Grant areas will be constructed in this order.

Why is Symmetrical Broadband Internet Superior?

Symmetrical broadband provides equal upload and download speeds, ensuring consistent performance even during peak usage. This means faster file sharing, reduced latency for a smoother gaming experience, and stable, high-quality streaming for both live and on-demand content. With symmetrical fiber internet, you can enjoy seamless connectivity for all your online activities without interruption.

Is Your Gamer Stealing All the Bandwidth?

 Nothing disrupts home peace like a bandwidth thief! With BrightRidge Broadband, you can put those worries to rest. Enjoy speeds starting at 300 Mbps for both uploads and downloads, allowing you to stream shows, game in Fortnite, and listen to music simultaneously without interruptions. Everyone can connect without worrying about slowdowns, so everyone stays happy online!
