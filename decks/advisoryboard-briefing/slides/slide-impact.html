What's at stake.

Financial: priced within range, but not a premium.
$14.5M cash equates to ~3.2x trailing revenue. Within the 2.8–4.5x range we've seen for comps in the last 18 months — but not a premium outcome. The $3M earn-out is contingent on Pendant's targets, which we don't yet have visibility into.
Strategic: distribution upside, brand downside.
Pendant has 7x our customer base and an established West Coast presence — both real distribution wins. But Vandelay would become a division inside Pendant, not a brand. Our team would lose the autonomy that's been part of how we've recruited.
Time: the 14-day window precludes a market test.
The exclusivity prevents us from re-engaging the two strategics who expressed interest in H1 (TKO Logistics, a regional PE shop). Either we accept this offer at face value, or we test the market without Pendant in it.
