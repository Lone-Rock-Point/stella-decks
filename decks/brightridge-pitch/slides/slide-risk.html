Risk & Communication

Risks we see. And how we'd handle them.

Content audit reveals more than 60 pages.
We catch this in week one, not week ten. Scope and timeline adjust together with you — no last-minute scrambles at the end of design.
SmartHub and form integration surfaces unknowns.
We prototype each integration in week two, before design locks. Problems surface early while there's still time to choose a different path.
Accessibility remediation uncovers deeper markup debt.
The theme-layer rebuild absorbs most of this. If deeper issues emerge in legacy content, we flag them with a proposed scope adjustment before committing.
How we communicate when things change.
Weekly status call, every Monday. Shared dashboard, always current. Issues surfaced within 24 hours with a written plan inside 48.
