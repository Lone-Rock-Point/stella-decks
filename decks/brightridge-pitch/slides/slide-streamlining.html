Information Architecture

Four ways to cut friction.

Persistent availability check.
Top-right of every page, not just the homepage. Prospects can check fiber availability from anywhere on the site without backtracking.
Language toggle in the header.
Not buried in the footer. Spanish-speaking customers see Spanish the moment they arrive — especially on mobile, where the footer is a scroll away.
Emergency banner above the header.
One toggle, activated by your team in seconds. Dismissable per user. Replaces the two-plugin notification setup that's there today.
Site search in the header.
Scoped to BrightRidge content only. Surfaces nutrition labels, support articles, FAQs. Analytics feed what to improve next.
